About us

SevenApps is a Russian casual game developers situated in Novosibirsk. We are a team of enthusiastic professionals and are continuously improving our games and creating new ones. We work in an open and friendly environment and cherish individual freedom and responsibility. We strongly believe the only way people can be truly creative is if they are enjoying what they do and are proud of what they make. This mindset helps us to create successful and high quality games.
